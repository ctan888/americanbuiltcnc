Why a shop would care

Not software as a pitch. Fewer ownership headaches.

The machine has to win on price and spec first. The service layer makes the buying decision feel less risky.

Parts in the U.S.

Consumables and critical spares stocked domestically so small shops are not waiting weeks for basic fixes.

Support that sees the machine

Remote log review and guided troubleshooting to reduce the number of problems that become site visits.
